Reflection

At first, I found it hard coming up with ideas that I would be interested in for the whole semester. I was more of a listener in the group sessions until I got home and talked to my family they really helped me out thinking of ideas. I also found it hard thinking of ideas because I was more worried about presenting the project at the end of the semester which really limited my ideas. Finally, at the end of the brainstorm I came up with 4 ideas that I would be interested to learn about and present, which were music, mental health, basketball and photography.
I decided to combine two of my brainstorm ideas, music and mental health. So, for my FYI project I chose to research the impact of mood when you listen to music. At the start I was excited to research and learn more about the two areas but I as I got deeper into the semester it got a bit boring as I had researched so much on my topic and then take notes and finally put it in a website, I found it a bit reparative. Note taking I found the hardest as I had to put all the information in short hand without copying most of the website. I found this hard as when I went back to my notes to write my website some of my notes didn't make any sense whatsoever so I had to then go back to my website.
I sometimes found making the website boring but the information on my big question got drilled into my brain and I would have not had the self-discipline to fully research a topic and fully understand. A lot of my work was done at school during the double periods on Friday afternoons, but sometimes I didn't get much work done. I found it easier to get work done by listening to music as it drowned out other noises so I could focus on my website and project.
